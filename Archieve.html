Archieve


Livestock disease in Ethiopia (LDE) project is based on a systematic map dataset provided by SEBI team, which contains 716 articles related to livestock diseases in Ethiopia from 2010 to 2019. The archive shows data visualizations indicating crucial features and findings in the dataset.
