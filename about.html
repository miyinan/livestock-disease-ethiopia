About


Explore livestock disease data in Ethiopia in an engaging way.




Background


Ethiopiaâ€™s livestock sector supports the livelihoods of millions of smallholder farmers. However, despite the improvements of recent years, livestock productivity remains low due to critical constraints, such as infectious diseases. We believe that having an overall and clear understanding of livestock disease data can help with future decisions and actions.







Ourwork

Livestock disease in Ethiopia (LDE) project is based on a systematic map dataset provided by SEBI team, which contains 716 articles related to livestock diseases in Ethiopia from 2010 to 2019. With interactive visualizations and narratives, we illustrate the current landscape of livestock disease prevalence and mortality, highlight areas for future attention and communicate the findings to the public in an engaging way.




Graph Interpretation


By state, you can check the number of studies of every disease of every species. Taking Trypanosomisis in Benishangul-Gumuz as an example, the block in the left lower corner shows that there are 65 studies of Trypanosomisis on cattle, while the color green shows it is a disease suffered by all four species.
